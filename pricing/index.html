Pricing Questions

Common questions about Typeflows pricing and licencing

Typeflows is completely free during Early Access and Beta for all users - including individuals, open source projects, and commercial teams. There are no limitations, seat restrictions, or usage caps during these phases.

See the Typeflows Beta Licence for full terms and conditions.

Pricing will begin at version 1.0.0, after both Early Access and Beta phases are complete. Details to be determined.

Yes, absolutely. Open source and non-profit projects will remain free forever, even after Early Access and Beta end at v1.0.0. This is part of our commitment to supporting the open source community.

We're still finalizing pricing based on beta feedback. Details will be announced well before v1.0.0.

We're committed to ensuring a smooth transition. Details about version compatibility and upgrade paths will be provided as we approach v1.0.0. Our goal is to make any transition as seamless as possible.
